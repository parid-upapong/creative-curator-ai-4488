# Strategic Roadmap: Global Creative Platform Standards (2024-2026)
**Project:** Creative Unlock AI
**Role:** OVERLORD (CEO)

## Vision
To redefine the global creative economy by establishing a standardized AI-driven interface that bridges the gap between raw creative talent and high-value professional opportunities.

## Phase 1: Foundation & Standard Establishment (Q3 - Q4 2024)
- **Unified Creative Schema:** Develop a proprietary data format (JSON-C) for creative portfolios that AI can parse with 99% accuracy.
- **MVP AI Pitch Engine:** Launch the "Instant Pitch" feature, converting static portfolios into dynamic, client-specific presentations.
- **Beta Testing:** Onboard 1,000 top-tier designers and copywriters to train the local alignment models.

## Phase 2: Global Integration & Intelligence (Q1 - Q2 2025)
- **Cross-Border Career Engine:** Integration with global job boards (LinkedIn, Behance, Dribbble) via automated API synchronization.
- **Context-Aware Matching:** Deploy LLMs trained on historical hiring data to predict "Client-Creative Fit" scores.
- **Multilingual Presentation Layer:** Automatic localization of creative pitches into 10+ major languages including Japanese, French, and Mandarin.

## Phase 3: Ecosystem Dominance (Q3 2025 - Q4 2026)
- **Standardization Certification:** Launch "AI-Verified Creative" badges, becoming a prerequisite for global enterprise hiring.
- **Real-time Skill Gap Analysis:** AI-driven dashboard for creators showing real-time global demand trends and suggested portfolio pivots.
- **The "One-Click" Hire:** Automated contract and escrow integration for instant global employment.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
1. **Presentation Speed:** Reduce pitch creation time from 4 hours to < 30 seconds.
2. **Success Rate:** Increase the interview-to-hire ratio by 40%.
3. **Global Reach:** 50% of platform revenue generated from cross-continent matches.
