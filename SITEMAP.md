# Sitemap: Creative Unlock AI Platform

## 1. Public Facing (Unauthenticated)
- **Landing Page:** Value prop, "The Future of Creative Work," and CTA.
- **Explore Talent (Public View):** Curated feed of top-tier creative works.
- **Pricing:** Subscription tiers (Freelancer vs. Agency vs. Enterprise).
- **Resources:** AI in Creativity Blog & Case Studies.

## 2. Creative Talent Portal (Authenticated)
- **Dashboard:** Overview of active pitches, profile views, and recommended jobs.
- **My Portfolio (The Engine):**
    - Project Uploader (AI-assisted)
    - Schema Editor (Refining AI extractions)
    - Style DNA (Visual mapping of the user's aesthetic)
- **Job Discovery:**
    - Smart Feed (Ranked by AI Match Score)
    - Saved Briefs
- **Pitch Lab:**
    - Active AI Proposals
    - Template Management (Dynamic styles)
- **Analytics:** Conversion funnel for applications.

## 3. Employer / Brand Portal (Authenticated)
- **Dashboard:** Active job postings, applicant pipeline, and AI "Top Picks."
- **Talent Scout:**
    - AI Search Bar (Natural language queries like "Find me a minimalist UI designer who understands fintech").
    - Shortlist / Collections
- **Job Management:**
    - AI Brief Builder (Helps employers write better job descriptions).
    - Interview Scheduler.
- **Contracts & Payments:** Escrow and milestone management.

## 4. Admin / Overlord Console
- **Platform Health:** AI model performance and processing queues.
- **Content Moderation:** Flagging inappropriate or AI-generated spam.
- **User Management:** Support and verification tools.
